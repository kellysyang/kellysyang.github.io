ClosetCore

An app integrating personalized fashion styling with socially conscious suggestions.



UX TECHNIQUES: Contextual Inquiry, User Personas, Information Architecture, Wireframing, Prototyping, User Testing

DURATION: Sep 2020 - Oct 2020 (1 month)



ClosetCore is the key to a feel-good wardrobe. ClosetCore integrates personalized fashion styling with socially conscious suggestions to empower people to treat their bodies and the environment with the respect they deserve. Users are able to select specific styles or aesthetics that interest them as well as causes they are most interested in considering while shopping. From there, they are recommended the suggested items they need to build their dream wardrobe and links to buy them from select brands.

research

I interviewed various people who frequently online shop about their goals and challenges when online shopping.

"I mainly online shop for instant gratification. I like browsing through the sales sections to find cheap, stylish items. I really want to expand my style, but sometimes it’s difficult knowing where to begin."

“I don’t typically consider social issues when online shopping, but definitely want to start. There’s just the convenience of not caring and it’s usually cheaper.”

style guide

overall design: ClosetCore is designed as a mobile application to cater towards user needs for convenience and easy access to the instant gratification of online shopping. The neutral colors represent the environmentally conscious nature of the app and the simple color palette reminds users that shopping wise can be easy.

product page: From the shop page, users are able to go into product detail pages and make purchase selections. They will then be redirected to the clothing brand shop.

folders: Users can organize products they like into folders or view all the items that have favorited. They will also receive notifications when favorited items are on sale.

profile: The profile tab allows users to view and name their past customized search results and also to view and see the status of past orders.
