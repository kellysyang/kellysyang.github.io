About Us

Established in 1990, Bun Bun Bake Shop is a family bakery based in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. We are a small bakery that specializes in cinnamon rolls, from our famous original cinnamon rolls to a variety of artisan flavors. Head chef Ammar has worked tirelessly to perfect each recipe with the help of his wife Joanne. Taste the authenticity and love we bake into each bite.
